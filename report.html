Introduction

Efficient market financial hypothesis thinks the market price could reflect all available information in the market, but in crypto-currency market, we find that some Key Opinion Leaders’ arbitrary comments may influence the price. Among them, Elon Musk is the most powerful KOL. His words can unleash a storm in the market. Is Musk’s impact value-motivated or noise trading? Contrary to the prior investigation on the rise and fall, we focus on the variability risk.
